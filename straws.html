Eco-Friendly Alternatives for Plastic Straws

Stainless steel/glass/bamboo straws

From restaurants to coffee shops, plastic straws are one of the most common single-use plastics. Often, straws are unnecessary yet always provided to customers. Luckily, can be replaced very easily and with time, you'll find yourself saying no to plastic straws. In addition to being bad for the planet, they have harmful toxins that can affect the taste of your drink and even make you sick.

Some of the most common replacements for plastic straws are stainless steel, glass, and bamboo straws. To find out more about why plasic straws are bad for the environment, check out this article.
