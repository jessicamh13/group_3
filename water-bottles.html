Eco-Friendly Alternatives for Water Bottles

Reusable Water Bottles

The most common way to reduce plastic water bottles is reusabale water bottles. Why waste all of that plastic for a one time uses that most people dont even end up recycling? If you want to learn how one time use water botles add so much unnecesasary plastic to the world, click this article.

Interested in learning more about why plastic water bottles are bad for the environment? Watch this video to learn about the life cycle of plastic that is used every day.


Some colleges and universities have implemented programs at their schools that are centered aroung switching from plastic to reusable water bottles. At the University of Florida, students can ask for barcodes that are scanned each time they fill their water bottle up at a refill station. After a student scans a certain number of times, they are eligible to win prizes for being good to the planet.
