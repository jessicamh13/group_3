Eco-Friendly Alternatives for Utensils

Bamboo Utensils

Every fast-food restaurant offers a multituude of plastic utensils for their customers, but at what cost? Although it may be easier for restaurants becuase it lessens the cleaning process and expedites service, but it's taking a toll on our beatiful planet. Although they may not seem like a pressing issue, plastic utensils are bad for the environment yet easily replaceable in day-to-day life.

Instead of picking up plasticware for convenience, consider taking your own utensils to restaurants that you know use disposable utensils. When it comes to packing your own lunch, consider taking the utensils that you use at your house or purchase some specifically for on-the-go use. To learn more about why plastic utensils are harmful to our planet, check out this article.
