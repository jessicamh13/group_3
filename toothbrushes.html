Eco-Friendly Alternatives for Toothbrushes

Biodegradable Toothbrushes

Did you know any toothbrush ever made is still out there in the world, and that they will never be "away".

1 billion toothbrushes are thrown away every year, that is enough to stretch around the world four times. FOUR.

We all toss our plastic toothbrush every three months and pop open a new one thinking nothing of it. Some toothbrushes find their way into the ocean and fish can mistake them for food. Make a simple change how you brush your teeth, and click here for more information.
