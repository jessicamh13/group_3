Eco-Friendly Alternatives for Plastic Grocery Bags

Reusable Grocery Bags

With each trip to the grocery store, dozens of bags are used and then discarded. They are often only used for about ten minutes and most users throw them away immediately after use without a single thought of recycling them.

Some stores have started charging their customers for using plastic bags, which incentivises customers to bring their own bags and refrain from using single-use plastic. Most stores sell multiple styles and types of reusable grocery bags, so you're bound to find one that you'll want to use over and over!
