Eco-Friendly Alternatives for Plastic Cups

Reusable cups

Reusable coffee cups can be found at most grocery and retail stores, and although they may seem expensive or inconvenient, they are very eco-friendly and last for years of use. Most coffee shops offer discounts to customers that bring reusable coffee cups to their shops. Not to mention, most reusable coffee cups are more aesthetically plesing than paper or plastic cups and can easily inspire others to make the switch to reusable cups.
